Capabilities

Criminal Law

Our criminal defense team provides aggressive representation for individuals facing charges ranging from misdemeanors to serious felonies. Whether you’re dealing with drug crimes, DUIs, violent offenses, or other allegations, we work to protect your rights, challenge evidence, and seek the best possible outcome—whether through negotiation, dismissal, or trial.

Family Law

Navigating family legal matters requires both sensitivity and expertise. We assist clients with divorce, marriage law, property division, and other family-related disputes, ensuring fair resolutions while minimizing emotional strain. Our goal is to guide you through complex legal processes with clarity and compassion.

Immigration

Immigration law is complex and constantly evolving. We help clients with asylum claims, family-based petitions, and other immigration matters, providing personalized strategies to overcome legal hurdles. Whether reuniting families or securing protection, we advocate for your right to a stable future in the U.S

Personal Injury

Accidents can leave lasting physical, emotional, and financial burdens. Our firm fights for victims of car crashes, motorcycle accidents, dog bites, brain injuries, and drunk driving collisions. We focus on proving liability, maximizing compensation, and ensuring you receive the justice and recovery you deserve.
